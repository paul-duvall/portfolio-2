About me

My name is Paul Duvall and I'm a professional individual determined to successfully transition into career in web development.

Right now, I'm working as a primary school teacher. It's a job that has challenged me to grow in many ways and provided me with opportunities to develop a broad range of skills.

I have always had an interest in technology and last year decided to dedicate my time to learning the skills necessary for front-end web development. I believe that these skills, along with my broader professional skills developed during my career as a teacher and my work in a online editorial role in London, will enable me to excel in a web-development environment.
