Hello there!

Welcome to my portfolio site - thanks for popping by! I'm Paul Duvall, a self-taught web developer based in Norwich, Norfolk. I've been working hard, learning a range of technologies that will enable me to produce visually-appealing, responsive web pages and applications.

Whilst you're here, you can find out more about what I do and my story. If you like what you see and would like to catch up with me about a possible project or work opportunity, feel free to contact me.
